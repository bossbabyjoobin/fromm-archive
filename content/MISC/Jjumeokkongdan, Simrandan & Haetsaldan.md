
# Fandom names

### JooBin

#### 쭈먹콩단 (Jjumeokkongdan) | 240107 – 240715

###### Breakdown 
**주먹**밥 (Jumeokbap) means rice ball in English
**콩** (Kong) means Bean in English (Bean ➡️ Joo**Bin**) 
**-단** is a suffix that means "group"

At one point, she even changed her fromm nickname to **콩** (Kong)
https://x.com/bossbabyjoobin/status/1766146651922583701

###### Origin
Jjumeokkongdan became her first fandom name because her tripleS profile portrait (which she drew herself) looks like a rice ball (주먹밥) 

![[tsprofile_bin.png|350]]
She just combined **주먹**(Jumeok) + **콩**(Kong) + **단**

#### 심란단 (Simrandan) | 240715 – Present

###### Breakdown 
**심란** means unsettled or uneasy
**-단** is a suffix that means "group"

###### Origin

This one’s a bit hard to explain without context, specifically JooBin’s fromm. If you’re not subscribed there, it might not be easy to fully grasp but here’s one instance that could give you an idea. 

One time, Binnie squeezed a bottle of hair essence but the opening was facing the wrong way. Instead of landing on her hands, it was all over her clothes and arms. In response, a WAV said:

>"As expected, we really should be 심란단 (Simrandan) rather than 쭈먹콩단 
(Jjumeokkongdan)" 

and Binnie replied:

>"I slighty agree"
